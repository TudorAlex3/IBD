Dataset Overview

The Coupon Purchase dataset contains information about coupon purchases, including details such as Coupon UPC, Product ID, and Campaign ID. This dataset can be utilized for analysis of purchasing behaviors, campaign effectiveness, and trends in coupon usage.

Columns

  • COUPON_UPC: Unique identifier for the coupon.
  • PRODUCT_ID: Unique identifier for the product associated with the coupon.
  • CAMPAIGN: Identifier for the marketing campaign linked to the coupon.

Explanation

This dataset is commonly used for marketing analysis, understanding consumer purchasing behavior, and optimizing promotional strategies through detailed analysis of coupon usage and its impact on sales and campaign performance.
