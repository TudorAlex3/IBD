Data Analysis Summary

This analysis examines the Black Friday sales dataset, with a particular focus on identifying and retaining outliers. Outliers are values that significantly differ from other observations in the dataset and may indicate unusual sales events, promotional strategies, or customer behaviors. By keeping these outliers, we gain a broader understanding of the extremes within the data, which might represent either errors or rare, significant events that impact sales patterns during Black Friday.
