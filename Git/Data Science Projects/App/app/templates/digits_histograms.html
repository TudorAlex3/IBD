Dataset Description

The Identify Your Digits dataset contains visual histograms of numerical columns, specifically focusing on digit recognition. The histograms illustrate distributions of pixel intensities for images associated with digit classes ranging from 0 to 9.

Histograms Overview

The visual representations below provide insights into the frequency distribution of pixel intensities for various digit classes:

  • Digit 0: Histogram shows low intensity areas around the borders with higher intensities in the center.
  • Digit 1: High concentration in lower intensity values, with distinct peaks.
  • Digit 2: Notable spread in medium intensity values.
