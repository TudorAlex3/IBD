Histograms for Recommendation Engine Dataset

Data Summary

Dataset Information: The Recommendation Engine dataset captures user interactions with products, including ratings, views, and engagement:

  • USER_ID: Unique identifier for each user interaction.
  • PRODUCT_ID: Unique product identifier associated with each user interaction.
  • RATING: Rating provided by users for products in the range [1, 5].
  • VIEW_COUNT: Number of times a product has been viewed.

Explanation of Histograms

The histograms illustrate the distribution of numerical features in the dataset. Key observations include:

  • RATING: Most users provide a rating of 4 or 5, indicating a positive experience, with fewer users rating lower.
  • VIEW_COUNT: The distribution shows a peak around lower view counts, with some products receiving high numbers of views.
  • PRODUCT_ID: Common product IDs show higher frequencies, whereas less popular products have lower engagement.
