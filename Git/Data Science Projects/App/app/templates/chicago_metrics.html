Model Metrics - Chicago Crimes Dataset

Explanation

This chart showcases the metrics (Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score) for models applied to the Chicago Crimes Dataset:

  • Logistic Regression: Provides balanced performance across most metrics, suitable for overall crime classification.
  • Decision Tree: Simple yet effective for capturing key patterns in crime data, though it may slightly underperform in recall.
  • Random Forest: Demonstrates the highest consistency across all metrics, making it the best choice for handling the complexity of crime classification.

Conclusion: Random Forest is recommended for the Chicago Crimes Dataset due to its superior performance in accuracy, precision, recall, and F1 Score. Logistic Regression and Decision Tree also show strong results, but Random Forest excels in complex datasets.
