Data Summary

Dataset Information: The Coupon Purchase dataset captures information on coupon purchases, including product IDs and campaign types:

  • COUPON_UPC: Unique identifier for each coupon purchase.
  • PRODUCT_ID: Unique product identifier associated with each coupon purchase.
  • CAMPAIGN: Campaign identifier linked to the coupon.
