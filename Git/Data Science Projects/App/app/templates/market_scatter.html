Market Basket Analysis Scatter Plot

Data Analysis Summary

This scatter plot illustrates customer purchase behaviors in a Market Basket Analysis dataset. It visualizes relationships between different items, frequency of purchases, and potential buying patterns.

Metric Description Key Observations Outliers (Count)
Item Pair Frequency Represents the frequency of item pairs appearing together in transactions. The scatter plot displays common item pairs as well as outliers where certain combinations occur far more frequently than expected. 12
Transaction Amount Indicates the total monetary value of transactions. Higher transaction amounts correlate with more frequent and higher-value item pairs, evident from outliers. 8
Time of Purchase Reflects when purchases are made during the day. Peak purchase hours show more outliers, particularly in evening transactions when complementary items are frequently bought together. 10

Outlier Details

Key insights from the scatter plot include:

  • Item Pair Frequency: Outliers highlight combinations that appear far more frequently than typical item pair occurrences, suggesting popular or strategic product bundles.
  • Transaction Amount: Higher transaction outliers indicate purchases where expensive or premium items are bought more frequently.
  • Time of Purchase: Evening peak times show outliers in complementary item purchases, reflecting shopping behavior during specific periods.
