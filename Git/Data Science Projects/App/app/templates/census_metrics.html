Model Metrics Comparison - Census Dataset

Explanation

This chart showcases the metrics (Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score) for three models applied to the Census Dataset:

  • Logistic Regression: A reliable model that achieves balanced metrics, making it suitable for this dataset.
  • Decision Tree: While simpler, the Decision Tree achieves high accuracy, but its recall may vary slightly compared to Random Forest.
  • Random Forest: The most robust model, consistently achieving the best overall performance metrics.

Conclusion: The Random Forest model is recommended for the Census Dataset due to its superior performance across all metrics. Logistic Regression and Decision Tree also perform well but may not capture intricate patterns as effectively.
