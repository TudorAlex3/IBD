Explanation

The BigMart Sales dataset contains 8,523 rows and 11 columns. Each row represents a product at a particular store, with various attributes such as:

  • Item_Identifier: Unique identifier for each product.
  • Item_Type: The category of the product (e.g., Dairy, Soft Drinks, Meat).
  • Item_Fat_Content: Indicates the fat content of the product (Low Fat, Regular).
  • Item_Weight: The weight of the product in kilograms.
  • Item_Visibility: The percentage visibility of the product in the store.
  • Item_MRP: The maximum retail price (MRP) of the product.
  • Outlet_Identifier: Unique identifier for the store where the product is sold.
  • Outlet_Type: The type of the store (e.g., Supermarket, Grocery Store).
  • Outlet_Location_Type: Indicates the store's location type (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3).
  • Outlet_Establishment_Year: The year the store was established.
  • Item_Outlet_Sales: The total sales of the product in the store (target variable).
