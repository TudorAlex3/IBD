Column Details

  • Item_Identifier: Unique ID of each product (Categorical, 1559 unique values).
  • Item_Weight: Weight of the product (Numerical, 415 unique values).
  • Item_Fat_Content: Fat content level of the product (Categorical, 5 unique values).
  • Item_Visibility: Visibility of the product in the store (Numerical, 7880 unique values).
  • Item_Type: Type of product (Categorical, 16 unique values).
  • Item_MRP: Maximum Retail Price of the product (Numerical, 5938 unique values).
  • Outlet_Identifier: Unique ID of the outlet (Categorical, 10 unique values).
  • Outlet_Establishment_Year: Year the outlet was established (Numerical, 9 unique values).
  • Outlet_Size: Size of the outlet (Categorical, 3 unique values).
  • Outlet_Location_Type: Location type of the outlet (Categorical, 3 unique values).
  • Outlet_Type: Type of outlet (Categorical, 4 unique values).
  • Item_Outlet_Sales: Sales of the product in the outlet (Numerical, 3493 unique values).

Key Insights

The dataset provides detailed sales data that can be analyzed for:

  • Trends in product sales across different outlets.
  • Impact of outlet size, location, and type on sales.
  • Relationships between product visibility, MRP, and sales performance.
