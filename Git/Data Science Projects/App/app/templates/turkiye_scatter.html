Outlier Details

Key insights derived from the scatter plots include the following:

  • Number of Repeats: Outliers represent students who repeated evaluation behavior unusually frequently, particularly in items related to course engagement (Q19 and Q21).
  • Q14: Several extreme negative responses were observed, possibly indicating isolated dissatisfaction events that warrant further investigation.
  • Q20: Outliers reflect highly polarized feedback on facilities, suggesting these responses may vary by specific class or lab conditions.
