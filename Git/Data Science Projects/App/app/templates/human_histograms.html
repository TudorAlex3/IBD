Dataset Information

The Human Activity Recognition (HAR) dataset contains measurements from wearable devices (accelerometers and gyroscopes) that record various physical activities performed by individuals. The dataset is commonly used for classification tasks to predict activities based on sensor data.

Key Insights

Insights from the histogram and the dataset:

  • The body acceleration magnitude has a bell-shaped distribution, with most values centered around the mean (0.28).
  • There are some outliers on both the positive and negative ends of the acceleration spectrum.
  • Higher values may correspond to more intense physical activity.

Handling Missing Values

The dataset has no missing values, as it is pre-processed and cleaned before being made publicly available.
