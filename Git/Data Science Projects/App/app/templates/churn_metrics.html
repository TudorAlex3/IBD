Metrics Explanation - Churn Dataset

Key Metrics

The following metrics are crucial for understanding the dynamics of customer churn in the dataset:

  • Churn: Indicates whether a customer has left the service (Yes/No). This is the target variable and helps predict customer retention.
  • Dependents: Denotes if the customer has dependents (Yes/No). This influences churn patterns as family obligations may affect service usage.
  • Gender: Provides demographic insights. While gender itself may not always directly affect churn, patterns can emerge when combined with other factors.
  • Partner: Indicates if the customer has a partner (Yes/No). Customers with partners may show higher retention due to shared service usage.
  • PhoneService: Shows if the customer subscribes to a phone service (Yes/No). This directly impacts their likelihood of staying with or leaving the service.

Analysis Summary

By analyzing these metrics, we gain valuable insights into factors influencing customer behavior. Outlier detection and removal can help refine predictive models for better accuracy and reliability.
