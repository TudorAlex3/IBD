Metrics Comparison - Coupon Purchase

Explanation

This chart showcases the comparison of key metrics (Precision, Recall, F1 Score, AUC) for different models applied to the Coupon Purchase dataset:

  • Logistic Regression: Provides a solid baseline performance, with balanced precision and recall.
  • Decision Tree: Offers good interpretability, achieving high accuracy, but can be sensitive to overfitting.
  • Random Forest: Demonstrates the best performance across all metrics, providing robust results even in complex scenarios.

Conclusion: The Random Forest model is recommended for the Coupon Purchase dataset due to its superior performance across multiple evaluation metrics. While Logistic Regression and Decision Tree are useful, they may not consistently outperform Random Forest in all scenarios.
