Column Details

  • type: Type of wine (e.g., red or white) (Categorical, 2 unique values).
  • fixed acidity: Concentration of fixed acids in the wine (Numerical, 106 unique values).
  • volatile acidity: Concentration of volatile acids in the wine (Numerical, 187 unique values).
  • citric acid: Concentration of citric acid in the wine (Numerical, 89 unique values).
  • residual sugar: Concentration of residual sugar in the wine (Numerical, 316 unique values).
  • chlorides: Chlorides content (Numerical, 214 unique values).
  • free sulfur dioxide: Free sulfur dioxide content (Numerical, 135 unique values).
  • total sulfur dioxide: Total sulfur dioxide content (Numerical, 276 unique values).
  • density: Density of the wine (Numerical, 998 unique values).
  • pH: pH of the wine (Numerical, 108 unique values).
  • sulphates: Sulphates content (Numerical, 111 unique values).
  • alcohol: Alcohol content (Numerical, 111 unique values).
  • quality: Quality score of the wine (Categorical, 7 unique values, rated from 3 to 9).
