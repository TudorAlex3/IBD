Market Basket Analysis Histograms

Data Summary

Dataset Information: The Market Basket Analysis dataset contains transactional data that captures customer purchases along with associated products:

  • Item Count: Numerical, representing the total number of items purchased in each transaction
  • Product Category: Categorical, showing various product categories (Groceries, Electronics, Clothing, etc.)
  • Transaction Amount: Numerical, the monetary value of the transaction

Explanation of Histograms

The histograms visualize the distribution of purchasing behavior in Market Basket Analysis. Key observations:

  • Item Count: Most transactions involve a moderate number of items (10-20 items).
  • Product Category: The distribution reflects a variety of categories, with the highest concentration in groceries, followed by electronics and clothing.
