Metrics Comparison - COVID-19 Prediction

Explanation

This chart showcases the comparison of key metrics (Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score) for different models applied to the COVID-19 prediction dataset:

  • Logistic Regression: Provides a solid baseline performance, balancing precision and recall, though potentially limited by simplicity.
  • Decision Tree: Offers interpretability, achieving high accuracy, but may struggle with overfitting in complex datasets.
  • Random Forest: Demonstrates robust performance across all metrics, providing consistent results even in diverse and complex scenarios.
  • XGBoost: Enhances performance with advanced gradient boosting techniques, offering superior accuracy and robustness.

Conclusion: Random Forest and XGBoost are recommended for the COVID-19 prediction dataset due to their superior performance across multiple evaluation metrics. Logistic Regression and Decision Tree are useful, but may not consistently outperform Random Forest and XGBoost in all scenarios.
