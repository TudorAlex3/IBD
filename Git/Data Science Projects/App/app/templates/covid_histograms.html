Explanation of Histograms

The histograms visualize the distribution of key features relevant to COVID-19 predictions. Key observations include:

  • AGE_GROUP: Shows varying frequencies of infection across different age ranges, with peak frequencies in mid-age groups.
  • GENDER: Indicates a higher prevalence of infections in males compared to females.
  • LOCATION: Displays the geographical spread of infections, with certain locations having higher numbers of cases.
