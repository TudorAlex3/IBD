Urban Histograms

Data Summary

Dataset Information: The Urban dataset contains various numerical features related to urban planning, such as population density, land use, and infrastructure:

  • Population Density: Numerical, measured per square kilometer
  • Land Use: Categorical, representing different types of land use (Residential, Commercial, Industrial, etc.)
  • Infrastructure: Numerical, encompassing different infrastructure aspects such as roads, parks, etc.

Explanation of Histograms

The histograms visualize the distribution of urban features. Key observations:

  • Population Density: Most urban areas have a population density between 1500 and 3500 people per square kilometer.
  • Land Use: The distribution shows a dominance of residential land use, followed by commercial and industrial areas.
