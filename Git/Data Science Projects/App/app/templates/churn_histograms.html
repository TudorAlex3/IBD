Histograms for Churn Prediction Dataset

Data Summary

Dataset Information: The Churn Prediction dataset contains customer details and service usage data to predict customer churn. Key attributes include:

  • customerID: Unique identifier for each customer.
  • tenure: Number of months the customer has stayed with the service provider.
  • MonthlyCharges: Monthly payment made by the customer.
  • TotalCharges: Total payment made by the customer over their tenure.

Explanation of Histograms

The histograms illustrate the distribution of numerical features in the dataset. Key observations include:

  • tenure: Distribution indicates customer retention patterns, with a peak at shorter and longer tenures.
  • MonthlyCharges: Most customers have monthly charges clustered around a central range, with a few higher spenders.
  • TotalCharges: The distribution aligns with tenure and monthly charges, showing increasing totals over time.
