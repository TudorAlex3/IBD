Black Friday Dataset - Histogram

Dataset Information

The Black Friday dataset contains shopping transaction data, including information about customers, products, and their purchase amounts on Black Friday. This dataset is often used for predicting customer purchase behavior.

Key Insights

Insights from the histogram and the dataset:

  • The distribution of purchase amounts is right-skewed, with most transactions falling below the median.
  • A few customers made large purchases, as indicated by the long tail in the positive direction of the histogram.
  • Higher purchase amounts could be associated with bulk buying, high-ticket items, or more frequent purchases by certain customer segments.

Handling Missing Values

The Black Friday dataset contains missing values, especially in the demographic-related columns (e.g., age, occupation). These can be handled by removing or imputing the missing data during pre-processing steps.
